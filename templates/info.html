Info

Here, you can find instructions to play the strategy game of surrounded. What is so special about this game? Well, you can play against an AI that uses a Neural Network, made using Google's TensorFlow, and try to win!

Note: The Neural Network is still in progress. So, right now, the opponent only makes random moves. However, we are working on the AI.

How to Play

The objective of the game is to SURROUND an enemy tile.

The blue tiles are the tiles which you can control, and are called Ally Tiles.

The red tiles belong to your opponent, and are called Enemy Tiles.

The ally and enemy tiles can move to Empty Tiles, which are light grey in colour.

To move an ally tile, you need to first click on it, and then click on the empty tile where you want to move it to. Selected tiles will be higher than unselected tiles, and will have a shadow. To unselect a tile, you can just click on it again.

Tiles can move horizontally and vertically, 1 step at a time. The game ends when an ally or an enemy tile cannot move anywhere, because they are surrounded. Be careful not to surround your own tiles!

In this picture, the Allies win, because the enemy tile can't move anywhere. It is surrounded in 3 sides by Ally Tiles, and in the other side by an Enemy Tile. Remember, to start a new game, you can click on the logo in the header.
